### 2️⃣ **Research Paper**  
**File:** `Claudia Ziyi Wang - The Impact of Recreational Marijuana Legalization on Crime Rates.pdf`

An independent policy research project that examines whether recreational marijuana legalization affects crime rates across U.S. states.  
Key components of the paper include:

#### **🧪 Data & Methodology**
- 13-year, 50-state **panel dataset**  
- Key variables: violent crime, property crime, legalization timing  
- Econometric approach: **fixed-effects panel regression**  
- Robustness checks and policy interpretation  

#### **📊 Main Findings**
- Legalization shows **no significant increase in crime rates** in most model specifications  
- Evidence suggests crime outcomes are more strongly driven by socioeconomic and demographic factors  
- The study highlights the importance of data-driven policy analysis  

#### **🎯 Why This Matters**
This research demonstrates my ability to:
- Work with panel data  
- Apply econometric modeling for policy questions  
- Interpret quantitative results clearly  
- Communicate insights to both technical and non-technical audiences
